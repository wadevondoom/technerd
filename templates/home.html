TECHNERDIAC

(tek-nur-dee-ak)

noun

Definition: A person who has an excessive and obsessive interest in technology, often at the expense of social interaction and physical activity.

Example: That person spends all day on their computer and never goes outside to exercise or meet people. They're a total technerdiac.

Etymology:

The term "technerdiac" is a combination of the words "technology" and "nerd" with the suffix "-iac" meaning "pertaining to" or "affected by".
