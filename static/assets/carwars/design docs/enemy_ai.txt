Useful information on creating enemy AI for top down 2d shooter games:
- Enemies should have a basic AI that allows them to move towards the player and attack
- Enemies should have different behaviors depending on their type (e.g. some enemies might be more aggressive than others)
- Enemies should have different strengths and weaknesses (e.g. some enemies might be more vulnerable to certain types of attacks)
- Enemies should be able to work together to create interesting and challenging gameplay scenarios

Types of enemies to include in the game:
- Basic enemy car that moves towards the player and attacks
- Fast enemy car that moves quickly and tries to ram the player
- Tank enemy that is slow but heavily armored and can fire powerful but slow projectiles
- Boss enemy that has multiple phases and requires the player to use different strategies to defeat
